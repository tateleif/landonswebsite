Armadillo

Type: Mammals

Armadillos live in the deserts of North and South America.


Armadillos are close related to sloths and anteaters. They have claws for digging and a hard shell to protect them from predators. The word armadillo means little armored one in spanish. Babies are called pups. Twins are common. Giant Armadillos are the largest species. Their toungs are long and sticky.

Fun Fact

Armadillos are the only living things with plates that cover their backs, heads and legs.
